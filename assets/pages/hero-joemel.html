Joemel - The Eternal Hashira

Once one of the deadliest Hashira, Joemel lived over five thousand years ago. He was a master of Stone Hardening and Foil Breathing techniques, wielding them with unmatched skill. Above all, Joemel possessed an extraordinary ability to adapt to any battle, making him an unstoppable force in the fight against darkness. His legendary prowess and adaptability have made him a figure of myth and inspiration for generations of warriors.

Joemel was also an Imaginary Trapper, a skill he acquired from his friend, Sivergel, who taught him this unique ability. This rare technique allowed him to manipulate and trap his enemies in powerful illusions, further solidifying his reputation as an invincible warrior.

One of Joemel's most fearsome abilities was Battle Adaptation: his capacity to learn and counter any opponent's fighting style, turning their strengths against them. However, due to his lack of full nen charge, Joemel was unable to fully utilize this skill, making it a dormant potential within him. Despite this limitation, his other abilities made him a formidable adversary. In his earlier days, Joemel was part of a legendary group called The Four, consisting of himself, Ryan, Vergel, and Angelo. They were inseparable friends and formidable warriors. However, a great misunderstanding and a deep-seated disagreement caused a rift among them. The once unbreakable bond shattered, splitting them into two factions: Joemel and Ryan on one side, Vergel and Angelo on the other. This division led to an epic conflict known as the 4th Impact.

Angelo, once a dear friend, became Joemel's main adversary. Angelo established a vast empire known as the Ays Empire, where he ruled with an iron fist. The battles between Joemel's and Angelo's factions were legendary, echoing through history as monumental clashes of power and skill.
